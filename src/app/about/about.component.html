The purpose of this development is to provide tutorials and ideas on UI aspects. Topics covered will be basically related to UI development, not service integration.

Sometimes, in pursuit of making a good UI with interactivity, developers often get inclined towards using ready-made UI frameworks or libraries such as Bootstrap, Google Material etc. Ofcourse, they come with their own features and benefits, however, one cannot ignore the load they put on the browser as well as the code. So, if we can achieve most of these tasks with plain and custom code, it will give us more control over our code.
